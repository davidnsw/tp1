About Me

简介

David Chen is an International Support Assistant in UON Global, International and Advancement Division at The University of Newcastle, Australia. As part of the UON Global team, he plays a critical role in answering enquires and supporting prospective international students with accepting an offer to study at the University. Before he joined UON Global, he was a visiting research fellow in the School of Architecture and Built Environment, UON. David now acts as a link for prospective students wishing to study engineering at UON and still does important research for the school of School of Architecture and Built Environment

Experience

Doświadczenie

David used to work in an E-commerce development and system integration company for three years, and accumulated a wealth of practical experience in this field. He spent four years of research in building environment (Construction site dust control), radiant energy intelligent refrigeration technology (2014 UON research project), and building materials (Self-healing of concrete) in China and Australia universities. He has published seven papers, one monograph: Analysis on the effects of the Three Gorges project on the climate of the Three Gorges Dam Reservoir Area. As a principal researcher, he participated in one China-Australian cooperative research project, two national projects, five provincial projects, and one enterprise-level project for a large e-commerce trading platform.
David hopes to combine these experiences and research topics with networking and professional development opportunities. David is very excited about the chance to explore suitable opportunities to make his own contribution to future economic and trade exchanges between China and Australia.
